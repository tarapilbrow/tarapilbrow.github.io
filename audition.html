Alameda-based dance company Tara Pilbrow Dance is auditioning for two dancers to join the company for the remake of Breathe, a piece first created in 2021 for 3 dancers.

Dancers should have a strong contemporary dance background, confident contact/ partnering skills, and be comfortable with improvisation and movement composition.

Performances of Breathe are planned for May 5/6 2024 at the Radium Runway outdoor performance venue in Alameda. Rehearsals will take place in Alameda 2-3 times per week between March and May - schedule to be confirmed with dancers.

Tara Pilbrow Dance is able to pay dancers as independent contractors, at $25/hr for rehearsals.

DAY: Friday, January 19th

TIME: 10am - 1pm

LOCATION: Rhythmix Cultural Works, 2513 Blanding Avenue, Alameda.
To be considered for the audition please send the following materials to contact@tarapilbrow.com

Intro letter, CV, and a short dance reel.

Materials are due January 5th, 2023 at 5pm

Applicants will be notified by Wednesday January 10th, 2023
Contact at contact@tarapilbrow.com
